Quick Troubleshooting

  • Force close and reopen the app.
  • Ensure you’re on the latest version from the App Store.
  • Check internet connectivity (Wi-Fi or cellular).
  • Log out and log back in (if applicable).
  • Restart your iPhone/iPad.
  • Check system date/time are automatic.
  • Allow notifications/media permissions if prompted.
  • For audio/video: verify device volume and silent switch.
  • Reinstall the app (this may clear local data).
